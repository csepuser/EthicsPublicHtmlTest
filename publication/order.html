Pricing and Delivery Options

Prices for all items listed below can be found on our List of Publications and Papers. This page can also be used to submit your order electronically. You may also request publications via regular mail by printing out the Ordering Publications form and sending it back to CSEP with the accompanying payment.

Orders can be sent by mail or delivered electronically.

For electronic delivery:

Please allow 2-3 business days to receive your order.

Setup fee for converting document into digital image: $5.00
plus original price of publication(s) found in the List of Publications

For delivery via regular mail:

Allow up to 4-5 business days for receiving orders within the US and 10 days for international orders.

Shipping and handling are applicable as follows:

$3.00 for all orders under 100 pages shipped in the United States and Canada.

For shipping internationally (Including Mexico)

$5.00 for orders of 1-25 page

$8.00 for orders of 25-50 pages

$10.00 for orders of 50-100 pages

For orders over 100 pages, CSEP will contact you with the amount due after receiving the order.

If you submit your request via the online form, we will contact you with the estimated price. If you send you order in via regular mail, payment must accompany your order.

PLEASE MAKE CHECK OR MONEY ORDER PAYABLE TO:
IIT Center for the Study of Ethics in the Professions.

Send all queries about publication order to csep@iit.edu or to:

Publication Orders
