The IEEE Trans on Software Engg is purely for research -related 'mumbo-jumbo'! Now a days you cannot read a single article without getting exasperated! I would go for the year-long option with the editor TROIKA ( Russian for a gang of three - me, you and X ! Could I look for X - I know a few in the Asia and Pacific Rim - a Japanese colleague?!). I would write an outline next week - in the midst of 'spring cleaning' in our apt and send it to you for comments!

I am balancing many things now.
