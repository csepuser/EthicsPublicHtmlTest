Agenda for Steering Committee Meeting on Feb 27 Email from Barbacci to Steering Committee: "Agenda for Steering Committee Meeting on Feb 27" - February 15, 1995 (Level 2) E-mail

This is a draft agenda. Please let me know if you would like to make
additions/changes.

If any of you has reading material to distribute to the committee,
please send it directly to the members address below or bring enough
copies (15) to the meeting. We might not have access to copying facilities in the Hotel.

Several of us will stay overnight. If you are free in the evening, we could get together for dinner. However, I am afraid I don't know any places in Nashville, recommendations for restaurants are welcome :-)

I'll see you on the 27th,

Regards,

Mario

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1:00 pm Welcome and introductions

1:10 pm Mario and Dennis Review charter and strategy Information for new members and verifying shared understanding of the approach

1:30 pm Stu Sweben and Joe Deblasi Report on the National Software Council Initiative and potential for cooperation/links.

2:00 pm Mario Contacts with British Computer Society and Institute of Electrical Engineers

2:15 pm Task Force Reports

4:00 pm Executive Session (Society appointees only, task force leaders are excused)

5:00 pm Adjourn
