Forum for Academic Software Engineering
(The Electronic Version)

Volume 3, Number 3, May 18, 1993 (FASE No. 13)

_____________________________________________________________________


1 Re: FASE No.12, Software Engineering As A Profession

2 Licensing And Certification Meeting

3 Faculty Position At University Of Houston - Downtown

____________________________________________________________________

1.
Just received my copy of FASE.12. Thanks for publishing my paper on the definition of software engineering as a profession and letting additional people know about it.

As an update:
The motion is scheduled to be presented for approval to the IEEE CS BoG on Friday, 21 May 1993 and I am making the rounds of the IEEE CS Boards and committees prior to that to gain insights from others.

I would be very glad to discuss it with any one or any group during the week of 21 May 1993 if that is desireable. For a group discussion, and if considered appropriate, I have some view graphs on the topic and would be glad to make those part of a presentation. The only qualifier may be that I some committments during that week to various BoG meetings.

Comments on the paper itself would be welcome (Laurie, I did not receive a copy of your memo that was referenced in FASE 12, and if you could send me a copy, it would be appreciated.)

We are going to actively seek a consensus of the concerned professionals in the field on this topic and will be (assuming the BoG approves the motion) energetically seeking participation from all those who are interested.

Fletcher
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

2.

Last week I sent out an announcement of a formal proposal for a standard to license software professionals. This proposal will be presented to IEEE board of GOV.S at the ICSE conference in Baltimore.

An informal lunch on friday 21 May at 12:15 is planned to discuss the licensing and certification. The lunch is not sponsored by ICSE, but by some peopleinterested in licensing & certification. We plan to meet at the
NICKEL CITY GRILL (201 EAST PRATT STREET, BALTIMORE 21202, 410 752 0900)
I will report to the list on this and other discussions about the licensing and certification

Don Gotterbarn
