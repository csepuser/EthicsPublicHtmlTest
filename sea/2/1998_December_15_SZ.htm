SWECC Meeting SWECC Meeting - December 15,1998 (Level 2) Scanned Document

Dave,

first of all, thanks for inviting me to the meeting with Dave Parnas on December 9, and for subjecting yourself once again to my passionate views about software engineering =) You mentioned to me in a phone conversation before the meeting that the Software Engineering Coordinating Committee (SWECC) would have its first meeting on January 28/29, would be meeting with the Texas Board on the morning of the second day, and you thought that it would be appropriate for the Board's Software Engineering Advisory Committee to also participate. I had lunch with Dennis and Laurie after the December 9 meeting, and he also expressed that it would be good for the advisory committee to be there. So, I will just go ahead and plan on being there on the morning of January 29, unless I hear differently before then.

Thanks again,

Don
