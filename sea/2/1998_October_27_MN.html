NEWS SOURCES:

Newspaper articles are easy to find and they give a brief synopsis of the
RstoryS. They do not often go into enough detail to use heavily in your
research papers and projects. Major newspapers usually go into more depth
than the local paper. Still as Information Technology (IT) issues become
increasing important the papers are devoting more space to these topics.
Monday is often Rcomputer dayS in newspaper Business sections. The NY
Times has recently added a RCircuitsS special computer news section each
Thursday as well. Major newspapers have added web sites where they often
go into more detail and/or have additional stories.
e.g. use specific newspapers such as the New York Times
www.nytimes.com
or their computer daily news service
http://www.computernewsdaily.com/
Other good candidates include: The Washington Post, The Wall St. Journal,
...

News magazines such as Time, Newsweek and US News and World Report
also cover computer ethics issues in nearly every issue. Time has a special
RDigitalS issue periodically which goes into additional depth on current topics
of interest.

Many other web sites are also available:
EXCITE http://nt.excite.com/ complete news sites
http://www.excite.com/news/technology/

sites
