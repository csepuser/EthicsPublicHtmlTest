Few people here appreciate the Tories Email from Gotterbarn to Jim (?): "Few people here appreciate the Tories" - 1997? (Level 2) e-mail

Dear Jim,

Few people here appreciate the Tories and now I am among those who don't appreciate them. John Major was worried about his standing so he called the election early to set up the longest campaign in English history. They close parliament during an election campaign, so I will not get to talk to "The Worshipful Company of IT", the information technology committee. It would have been great fun to have the guy from East Tennessee address "The Worshipful Company".

However, I was in the Netherlands on Tuesday and Wednesday meeting with their Office of Technology Assessment in the Hague. They are interested in information technology ethics. The bottom line is I am a co- director of an international computer ethics conference to be held in Rotterdam next March (don't panic- THEY are covering my expenses :- ). The neat part is that they are also willing to provide scholarships for two of our graduate students to attend the meeting. The scholarships will make all of the work related to the meeting worth it.

I have not heard from you regarding my request for some help with the three meetings in June. Terry and Peggy both have no funds available to help cover the other half of my costs.

I was on the Web today and visited the ETSU site. The pages have really improved congratulations.

best regards,

don
