Country: Great Britain Organization: IEE (Institute of Electrical Engineers) Individual(s): Speaker at PASE conference Steering Ream Representatives: Don Gotterbarn, chair of task force on ethics and professional practices Topics: body of knowledge / certification / ethics&practices / education Date of First Contact: 2/1/96 Summary of Interaction(s): - date 2/1/96 I did not get the same response from the IEE. Country: Great Britain Organization: CCSR Centre for Computing and Social Responsibility Individual(s): Simon Rogerson, Director Steering Ream Representatives: Don Gotterbarn, chair of task force on ethics and professional practices Topics: body of knowledge / certification / ethics&practices / education Date of First Contact: 2/1/96 Summary of Interaction(s): - date 2/1/96 Rogerson indicated a strong interest in the work of our task force.
