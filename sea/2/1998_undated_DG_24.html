Ballot Response Ballot Response and changes to code made in response- table - Undated, 1998 (Level 2) Electronic Document

No Text Ref Binding Comment Disposition/Action
1 General Yes

I have followed the format of the IEEE SPMP on a variety of projects, and all of these required the acquisition of third party software, and more often than not computer hardware (e.g. specialist terminals, file servers, PCs). There is only section 4.5.1.3 to define this, and I feel that as these are usually items that are shown as activities in the project plan schedule either as milestones, or activities there must be a separate section for these.

Suggestion: - Hardware Acquisition Plan Third Party Software Acquisition Plan Training Acquisition Plan Administrative and Other Services Acquisition Plan

A An additional sentence has been added to clause 4.5.1.3 indicating that clause 5.1.3 can be expanded to include additional subclauses of die form 5.1.3.x
2 General Yes

 

There is duplication of content of PMP and SQA plans, this needs to be sorted out, as on large projects both of these plans will be produced and it is not clear where the information should reside. Examples are Risks Management, Methods . Tools and Techniques, Problem resolution, supplier control/sub contractor management, metrics collection.

 

 N We will continue to resolve the duplications in subsequent versions of the standards.
