IMPORTANT ?? Please tell us about your specific interests and how
your experience will aid this project.

As President of the company, my interests cover all aspects of
Software Engineering as a profession. Our company is currently
providing pension and benefits software to large employers, as
well as consulting advice. Having us review other software
developers' work is a role that seems to be gaining popularity
with our clients.

I have worked in both large and small environments, and have
witnessed both noteworthy honesty and ethical conduct, and the
extreme opposite, by both software developers and end?users.

I can bring to the project a knowledge of the conflicts that arise
in a large corporate environment, where high billings sometimes
push against the boundaries of ethics. As well, as the President of
of a small firm, I can contribute knowledge of the problems and
solutions faced by small companies and independent developers.

Thank you very much for the opportunity to participate in these
working groups.

Rick

with the Population Commission for a year before I went to study

environments, and my education in a liberal arts setting (my
college degree was granted by a Jesuit institution) together with
my graduate training in computer science.
