Thank you for your response, Dr. Gotterbarn. To clarify my request, my team is working to enhance our existing guidelines for procurement professionals to be appropriate for a wider group within Dell. We would like to adopt some of the concepts of the Software Engineering Code of Ethics and Professional Practice for our guidelines, but it would not be a direct adoption (more conceptual). If it would make you more comfortable, we could send you a draft when it is ready for your review.
