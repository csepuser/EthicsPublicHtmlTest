Joint IEEE Computer Society and Association for Computing Machinery Steering Committee for the
Establishment of Software Engineering as a Profession

Mario R. Barbacci

March 1994

Background Mission Statement

"To establish the appropriate sets(s) of standards for professional practice of software engineering upon which industrial decisions, professional certification, and educational curricula can be based. "

Initial Recommendations (11/93):
1. Adopt standard definitions
2. Define Body of Knowledge and Recommended
Practices
3. Define Ethical and Professional Practices
4. Define Curricula
