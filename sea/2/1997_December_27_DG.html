Re: Ho, etc. Email exchange between Miller, Gotterbarn, and Rogerson: "Re: Ho, etc." - December 27, 1997 (Level 2) E-mail

Don:

I think it is important that people do get a response, and I hope we can make them feel part of the process. I suggest the following:

Answer for the three of us, since we have the authority to speak only for ourselves.

I suggest the following:

1. We make it clear that version 4.0 is up before IEEE and ACM governing bodies, and we cannot change it now. Send respondents a copy of version 4.0.

2. Tell people (and commit ourselves to the project) that we will begin work on a version 5.0 in 1998, that will take into account all the feedback we get between now and Thanksgiving 1998.

3. Write a proposal (to ACM, IEEE, NSF, ...) for us three to get together again in Dec 1998.

4. After our meeting, start suggestion the changes to the powers that be.

Keith
