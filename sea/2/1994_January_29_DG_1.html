II. Scope of Task Force

1. Codes of Ethics (not equal to) Standards of Conduct

Codes of ethics identify conflicts

Standards of conduct provide: Guidance to resolve conflicts (Players are users, developers, employers, regulators, penumbra) Standards of quality, and Responsibility for understanding the implications of one's work.

2. Cultural/National differences

3. Lead by appointed co?chairs (small task force, focus groups, surveys etc. in coordination with other task forces)

4. Standards development model as process III. Activities Prior to Meeting #1

2. Establish email, conferencing, repository 3. Initiate library search & examples (codes, ethics, licenses, certifications, PARS for related activities).
