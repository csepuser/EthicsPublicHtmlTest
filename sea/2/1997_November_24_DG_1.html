ACM / IEEE code of ethics Email from Carl Scheider to Gotterbarn: "ACM / IEEE code of ethics" - November 24, 1997 (Level 2) E-mail

Sir.

My company, The St. Paul Companies, would like to adopt the ACM / IEEE code for our internal use. We've modified it slightly to generalize the language beyond systems engineers.

If we were to take this approach, what kind of source reference or citation would the joint task force find appropriate.

We were in the midst of developing a code, and found your draft to be very helpful. We also think it has considerable additional strength when backed by these two prestigous societies. But . . . we found some of the wording cumbersome for employees who are not in a consulting arrangement, and for IT staff that are not doing systems engineering, but rather providing related services.

Any other concerns you might have? I could send you the "modified" draft, with the revisions clearly marked.

Thanks for your help.
