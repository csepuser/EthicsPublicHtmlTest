Cancelled the SEEP meeting Email from Gotterbarn to Melford: "Cancelled the SEEPP meeting" - January, 1994? (Level 2) E-mail

Bob,

I have canceled the SEEP meeting that you wanted set up for June at NECC. Only Joyce and Steve Barber responded that they could be there.

We have a major problem that needs to be addressed. We are just plodding along and have not given adequate direction advocacy or leadership by example to the group.

We schedule meetings but the preparation is not complete for the meetings and when we leave with tasks to work on between meetings they are not completed by the next meeting. With the exception of the draft CFP and the draft guide to operations, work seems to be done ONLY at the meetings.

My recollection of the meetings goes like this.

Jan 94- Pittsburgh, organizational meeting with Mario at SEI and set up plan.

Between meetings we worked and gathered recruits for working group leaders- some enthusiasm

APRIL 30 94- Washington get aquatinted meeting , define scope of task force -( written up at March 25, 1995 meeting) prepare CFP for distribution ( we didn't get to this). Plan to set up FTP site, and do relevant library searches. (This has fallen through the cracks)

JUNE 11, 94 Boston College- Although the CFP was not launched and thus we could not evaluate responses to it, we did the things necessary to launch the CFP- Productive meeting- set up working group categories had several SEEPPers ready to lead groups. Wanted to launch CFP and have names of people by Sept so we could meet in Nov 94 at Ethics in Computer Age Conference and discuss the assignment of working group members and the refine the directions for working group leaders so they could start their work/.

JUNE 21- Congressional briefing fiasco

NOV 11, 1994 Computer Ethics Conference- you me and Pat Sullivan in attendance. At this time we had only few responses and had not started on the guide for working group leaders.

Jan- February- You completed Draft version of Operations Guidelines. I sent some suggestions for modification and also 2/16 sent it to my working group saying we almost were ready to go, set up mail site for group etc..

February 95 At Meeting in Nashville of Steering Committee- you requested March 25 meeting of task force in Washington

March 25 Meeting in Washington (You me Steve and Keith) We completed wordsmithing the Operations Guidelines and the scope for the SEEP task force which is to be used as a model for the guiding the working groups in writing their scopes.

APRIL, New Orleans You asked me to set up a meeting for June 17 at the NECC conference. I invited the task force and received only two responses.

I think this response is due in part to the fact that they have received no real direction from us yet. We have meetings and articulate great plans but very li ttle happens. We have many volunteers from September-November of 1994 who are complaining wondering when, if, we are going to get started. Some of those who originally volunteered for working groups have already abandoned ship. I am afraid the same is happening with the task force itself. Please email me the material we worked up in Washington and I will write a cover document with some directions on how to get started, a plea to contact their groups, etc.

Don
