Narayana:

IEEE Software is undergoing many changes now. For example, lengths for all articles are being reduced to a maximum of 5 magazine pages (by reader request), number of papers per special issue is being reduced to 3 or 4 (again by redaer request). All articles will be VERY practical, with ideas that can be applied in the real world NOW (not just in 5-10 years).

We would certainly welcome a proposal from you on your selected topic provided it fits in with all of the above restrictions (als just ONE issue, not multiple issues). Lead guest editors for all future special issues will have to be from industry (there could sertainly be a second guest editor at a university), to further increase the probability of having only the most practical papers appearing in print.

Al
