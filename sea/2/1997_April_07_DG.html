A sample exercise with your draft SE code Email from Peter Knoke to Gotterbarn: "A sample exercise with your draft SE code" - April 9, 1997 (Level 2) E-mail

Don,

I've tried several exercises using your draft SE code with my class of 14 Computer Science seniors. These exercises included your suggestion to try to apply your code to selected parts of "Killer Robot".

Most recently, I asked the class to try to apply the code to Bill Gates. I thought this would get some reaction, because Gates and Microsoft seem to be widely disliked up here (not by me, of course).

Here is a sample response to that exercise. I have several others.

I'm grateful to you for helping me by sending the draft code, and I'd now like to give you the feedback you asked for. Do you have a preferred form for this? For example, will bits and pieces (like this one) be helpful, or would you prefer that I provide some kind of summarizing paper? (Option 1 gets quick data, option 2 takes longer).

I hope you're enjoying your stay in England.

Pete
